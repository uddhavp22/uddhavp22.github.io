EEG · Representation Learning · Neuroscience

Understanding EEG Foundation Model Representations

Foundation models trained on EEG signals are becoming increasingly capable — but what do they actually learn? This project probes the internal representations of EEG foundation models to understand whether they capture cognitively meaningful structure, or something more spurious. Draws on mechanistic interpretability methods and structured probing tasks across multiple cognitive paradigms.

Improving LAYA: EEG Foundation Models at Scale

Follow-up to the LAYA EEG foundation model paper. Investigates architectural and training-regime improvements to enhance representation quality and downstream generalization across EEG decoding tasks — with a particular focus on cross-subject transfer and scaling behavior. What makes an EEG foundation model actually general?

Music · AI · Self-Supervised Learning

Automated Music Arrangement with JEPA

Using Joint Embedding Predictive Architectures (JEPA) to learn rich representations of musical structure for automated arrangement. The goal: given a melody or solo piano piece, produce a coherent multi-instrument arrangement that preserves harmonic intent and phrasing. Motivated by the question of whether predictive self-supervised objectives can capture music's hierarchical structure.
