EEG · Representation Learning · Interpretability

Understanding EEG Foundation Model Representations

Foundation models trained on EEG signals are becoming increasingly capable — but what do they actually learn? This project probes the internal representations of EEG foundation models to understand whether they capture cognitively meaningful structure, or something more spurious.

EEG · BCI · Foundation Models

Improving LAYA: EEG Foundation Models at Scale

Follow-up to the LAYA EEG foundation model paper. Investigates architectural and training-regime improvements to enhance representation quality and downstream generalization across EEG decoding tasks — with a particular focus on cross-subject transfer and scaling behavior.

Music · AI · Self-Supervised Learning

Automated Music Arrangement with JEPA

Using Joint Embedding Predictive Architectures (JEPA) to learn rich representations of musical structure for automated arrangement. The goal: given a melody or solo piano piece, produce a coherent multi-instrument arrangement that preserves harmonic intent and phrasing.
