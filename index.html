hi, i'm uddhav.

I'm a researcher fascinated by how the brain encodes and represents information — and what we can learn about intelligence by studying it. My work sits at the intersection of representation learning, EEG, and brain-computer interfaces. I'm particularly drawn to foundation models for neural signals: what do they actually learn? Are the representations they build cognitively meaningful, or something more superficial?

Outside of research: I play piano with more enthusiasm than precision, cook and bake things of varying success, play chess and lose with dignity, and watch FC Barça with the full emotional range that implies. I'm a sucker for a great fantasy epic and have opinions about Plato.
