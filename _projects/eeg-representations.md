## motivation

Foundation models for EEG are getting better at downstream tasks — but we have very little understanding of *why*. What structure do they actually encode? Do their internal representations correspond to anything cognitively or clinically meaningful, or are they just useful statistical features with no interpretable structure?

This project is an attempt to open up that black box.
